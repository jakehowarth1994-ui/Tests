The Full Chain

Anthony Craig · Half Guard Pass → Mount → Back Control → Front Headlock Series
Entry — Niche-Old Half Guard
01 — Pass
Split Squat → Knee Slice
  • Lateral step to square opponent's hips
  • Roll elbow inside, establish underhook
  • Post into split squat passing position → sets up knee slice
  • Apply upper-body pressure — this baits a frame on your topside shoulder
↓ opponent frames on shoulder
02 — Punish the Frame
Rau Drag (Wing Chun Pass)
  • Use the frame reaction to hit the Rau drag
  • Ride it around the backside of opponent's body
↓
03 — Finish
Kimura
  • Finish from the backside position off the pass
Separate Entry — Mount
01 — Neutralize
Head & Elbow Control
  • Pick opponent's head off the mat — removes their ability to bridge
  • Scoop underneath both elbows
↓
02 — Advance
Double Underhooks → S-Mount
  • Upper-body pressure locks in underhooks on both sides, arms above opponent's head
  • Head scooped + both underhooks secured → sit into S-mount
↓
03 — Finish
Armbar (Either Side)
  • Finish depending on opponent's reaction
Separate Entry — Referee's Position
01 — Control
Seat Belt
  • Establish seat belt grip
  • Underhook arm must always cover the topside arm — that becomes the choke arm
↓
02 — Expose
Knee Ride → Peel the Ankle
  • Knee ride the hip
  • Peel the back ankle to expose opponent's chest to the ceiling
↓ opponent rolls over far shoulder
03 — Follow & Insert
Hook Insertion off the Roll
  • Follow the roll, insert leg hook on the same side the knee ride was on
  • Pull opponent across your body into back control
↓
04 — Finish Position
Body Triangle
  • Bottom-side arm = underhook, always. Top-side arm = choke arm, always.
RULE — this doesn't change: whichever arm is underneath is your underhook; whichever arm is on top is your choke.
Separate Entry — Front Headlock
Option A
Guillotine
  • Grab inside of your own thigh
  • Take slack out of your arms — shrinks the space the head/arm occupy
  • Crunch down to finish
Option B
Quarter Nelson → Darce
  • Transition off the quarter nelson grip into the Darce
Option C
Anaconda
  • Legs cover/trap opponent's tricep
  • Feed arm deeper through the choke
  • Feed back of opponent's head to your chest, fold neck down
